LEVEL 6

Level Six modules offer an increased degree of personal choice for the development of independent, high quality work, demonstrating the higher-level challenges of synthesis and innovation. In-depth research and design work is characterized by the ability to analyze, synthesize and apply creatively what has been learnt during the program of study.


The range of projects undertaken reflects the broad range of interests of the students within the course. They may include: typography and design for print; animation and visual narratives; exhibition and installation design, design for screen and interaction; and, signage, wayfinding, and environmental graphics.

Image: Daniele Polombo

MERMAID TEARS

Film sequences featuring abstract shapes and forms that are visually engaging and beautiful, but ultimately deliver a shocking and telling message about one of the threats to our environment. Mermaid Tears conveys thought provoking statistics on the effects of plastic waste in the oceans and its threat to the marine life that inhabit them.

Designer: Ola Liszewska
